# GitHub Profile Banner Design Concept & AI Prompts

A professional developer profile needs a stunning, personalized banner that immediately communicates their domain of expertise to recruiters and hackathon judges. Since your work bridges the physical (IoT, ESP32, sensors) and digital (full-stack web development, AI, Rust/JavaScript backends), your banner design is themed around **Cyber-Physical Systems & Edge Intelligence**.

---

## 🎨 Design Theme: "Bridging Code & Circuits"
- **Aesthetic**: Dark-mode, sleek, premium, high-contrast, sci-fi cybernetic workspace.
- **Color Palette**: Deep space black background (`#0d1117`), dark navy base, glowing cyan/teal for code/digital elements (`#00f2fe`), and electrical copper/amber for hardware/circuits (`#ff9f43`).
- **Visual Elements**:
  - **Left side**: Sleek glowing schematics of an ESP32 microcontroller, circuit tracks glowing with electric currents, and floating code blocks (NodeJS/Rust).
  - **Right side**: A stylized glowing dashboard displaying real-time graphs, sensors data, and a mesh network representing offline connectivity (MeshChat/RuView).
  - **Center**: Dark space, perfect for overlaying text or keeping it minimal and clean.
